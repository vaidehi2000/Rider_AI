1. What is a Robot?

A robot is a machine that can perform tasks on its own (or with some help) using:

  • Sensors: Like eyes or ears (e.g., cameras, LIDAR) to sense the world.
  • Actuators: Like muscles (e.g., motors, wheels) to move or act.
  • Control System: The ‚Äúbrain‚Äù that decides what to do (e.g., a computer program).

2. Sense-Plan-Act Cycle

  • Plan: Decide what to do (e.g., turn left to avoid an obstacle).
